Dlightful Web Redesign Case Study

Dlightful stories is a group of videographers and photographers that creates content primarily for digital use such as facebook cover video, video content for youtube, etc.

There’s an existing website but it was made as a temporary home page…

Understanding The Problem

We initially focused too much on the website and made assumptions on what our potential clients wanted to see like showing portfolios. During these discussions, we started talking about more broader questions like where do we want the business to go? What kind of projects and clients do we want? What is the purpose of this website? How do we know if the website is successful?

We started to understand our previous projects and clients Using Journey Map of our previous clients Create Personas to understand them more

Realization of where we wanted to go: Go to more brand videography so we can offer more types of video content and have more multi project deals with brands

Using Design Sprint Methodology

We realized our bigger questions can be answered using a Design Sprint *Short description of Design Sprint

New Project Goal

  • Clearly define our value/offer in a more specific way.
  • Define our ideal clients.
  • How to use the website to connect our offer to clients.
